Have a question? Check out the FAQ

What is the main objective of The Gnosis Future Skills Workshop?

The workshop aims to equip teenagers and young adults with practical, career-enhancing skills in areas like web development, graphic design, social media marketing, data analysis, and leadership.

Who is eligible to participate in the program?

The program is designed for teenagers and young adults, particularly students eager to develop essential skills for the digital age.

What makes this workshop different from other educational programs?

The Gnosis workshop emphasizes hands-on learning, real-world applications, project-based collaboration, and affordability through a revenue-sharing model.

What skills will participants gain from the workshop?

Participants will learn foundational digital literacy, creative design, project management, leadership, and teamwork, among other skills.

How long is the program, and what does it include?

The workshop spans 12 weeks, featuring interactive sessions, collaborative projects, and a Certificate of Completion for all participants.

How does the revenue-sharing model benefit schools and students?

This model ensures affordability for students while contributing to the school’s extracurricular funding, enhancing its reputation and educational offerings.
